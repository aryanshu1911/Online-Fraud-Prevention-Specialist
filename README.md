# 💸 Online Fraud Prevention & Awareness Repository

## 📌 Overview

This repository is a curated collection of real-world **online fraud case studies, scam breakdowns, and awareness reports**. It is designed to help users understand how modern cyber fraud works, how victims are targeted, and how such scams can be prevented.

With the rise of **AI-powered scams, deepfakes, UPI fraud, and digital impersonation**, online fraud has become more sophisticated, scalable, and harder to detect.

This project aims to build awareness through **structured case studies backed by real incidents and sources**.

***

## 🎯 Objective

- Document real-world cyber fraud cases in a structured format
- Break down scam execution step-by-step
- Identify psychological and technical manipulation tactics
- Provide prevention-focused insights
- Build awareness for students, professionals, and general internet users

***

## ⚠️ Why This Matters

Modern fraud is no longer simple phishing.

Today’s scams include:
- AI deepfake voice & video impersonation
- Digital arrest scams by fake police officers
- Crypto investment traps ("pig butchering")
- Fake job offer pipelines
- UPI & banking credential theft

These scams combine **technology + psychology**, making them extremely convincing.

***

## 🧠 Key Learning Areas

- Social engineering tactics
- Psychological manipulation techniques
- AI-powered fraud methods (deepfakes, voice cloning)
- Financial fraud patterns (UPI, crypto, banking)
- Real-world scam lifecycle breakdown
